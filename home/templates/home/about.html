About the Artist

The mind behind the mysticals

Joe Django

Joe Django is a digital artist working at the intersection of cute and dark — blending chibi character design with gothic atmospheres, neon mysticism, and surreal dreamscapes.

Every piece in the Molishi Mysticals collection tells a story of spirit worlds, ancient temples, and ethereal gatherings. The characters — small, wide-eyed, luminous — navigate landscapes that feel both familiar and otherworldly.

Joe creates limited-edition prints for collectors and offers bespoke commissions for those who want something truly personal. From icons and logos to large-format posters and portraits, every commission is crafted with the same attention to mood, colour, and narrative.

The Process

1. Concept

3. Render

Digital painting brings the scene to life with neon highlights and deep shadows.

4. Print

High-quality giclée printing on museum-grade paper, numbered and signed.
